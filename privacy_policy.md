# Privacy Policy for Course2Cal for RWeb

**Effective Date: March 11, 2026**

Course2Cal for RWeb ("we," "us," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
The Extension collects course information (course title, type, days, times, and location) from the UCR RWeb registration pages only when you explicitly click the "Scrape Courses" button.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
*   **Locally**: The scraped information is processed locally on your computer to prepare calendar events.
*   **Google Calendar**: When you click "Confirm & Sync," the Extension uses the Google Calendar API to create events in your primary Google Calendar.

## 3. Data Storage and Sharing
*   **No External Servers**: We do not send your data to any external servers. All processing happens within your browser.
*   **Third-Party Services**: The Extension interacts with Google APIs. Your data is handled according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
*   **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions:
*   `activeTab`: To read course info from the page you are currently viewing.
*   `identity`: To authorize with your Google account for calendar sync.
*   `storage`: To temporarily save the sync status.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository or Chrome Web Store support page.
